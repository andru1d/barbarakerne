policies

Confidentiality

The therapist~client relationship is protected by law. This means you have the right to confidentiality. I have the ethical and legal duty to protect your rights. There are limits to this regarding abuse, neglect, and in cases of harm to self or others. Confidentiality rights and limitations will be explained during informed consent at your first session, and at any time you have questions.

Sessions

Adult and Couple therapy sessions are 50 minutes.
Child and Play therapy sessions are 45 minutes.
Appointments are scheduled weekly. In some cases, this may vary depending on client needs and treatment plan. I need the 10 to 15 minutes between appointment times to make case notes, check voicemail, and return calls.

Insurance

I accept Blue Cross / Blue Shield, Cigna, Tri-care, Atnea, and Medicaid. I belong to these Employee Assistant programs: Cigna, ComPysch and Deer Oaks. If you want to use another health insurance provider, I will be happy to provide you with a statement to give to them for reimbursement. It is your responsibility to determine whether or not my services are covered by your particular insurance provider and plan.
As a courtesy to clients, I will gladly complete and file your insurance claims. If you have an annual deductible, you will be responsible for paying for services until it has been met. If you have any questions about fees, insurance coverage, or would like to make an appointment, please contact me: 979-473-9473 or shannonshannontherapy.com.

Cancellation Policy

Since scheduling of appointment reserves time specifically for you, a minimum 24 hours notice is required for rescheduling or canceling. The same day cancellation fee is $50.00. Insurance companies do not reimburse for missed sessions. I have the discretion to terminate counseling with clients who regularly miss appointments or fail to notify me regarding late arrivals. If you do not show up or cancel, and you have a regular weekly session time, you must contact me in order to hold that slot.
