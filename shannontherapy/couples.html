couples [services]
Couples therapy can be a powerful tool to assist your union through difficult times. Life events can change things. You may be dissatisfied in your love relationship. It can feel painful to feel disconnected from your spouse / significant other, or to be fighting all the time. I believe that with your joint effort and my help, you can rebuild your relationship into one that is filled with love and communication, autonomy, and a sense of connection.
I am trained as a marriage and family counselor. I focus on strengthening communication, emotional safety, and relationship balance. I can help to identify problematic roles and family of origin issues that challenge your current partnership.
I will work to help you, as a couple, define and refine your marriage/partnership goals and vision. We will explore relationship dynamics and recurrent themes. We will work to learn and practice effective communication skills.
We will build on your strength as a couple. We use the power of laughter, analysis, and mutual consideration.
I work with many types of couples: traditional and non traditional. GLBT couples are always welcome.
