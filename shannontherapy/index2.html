Psychotherapy
Shannon Taylor
I strive to create a validating relationship and a foundation of acceptance, so that my clients can explore, discover and grow personally. I focus on innate strengths through this idea: you have all the resources within to find your own best answers. My training and theoretical knowledge supporting me in guiding you on this journey.
I am deeply humbled and rewarded as a therapist at the sublime moments when I facilitate processes of self-discovery and healing. Whether I am working with adults, couples, whole families, or teens and children, I offer a warm, empathic approach. I believe in the power of acceptance and laughter to heal. My aim is to be strong and present while you reach the center of your sorrows, to create a safe and therapeutic environment fostering an easy-going atmosphere for all of us to grow, change, and co-create a happier life.
