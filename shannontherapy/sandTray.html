sand tray
Sandtray [The World Technique] is an expressive therapy method for all ages. You create patterns and "worlds" by arranging figurines amidst sand, sculpting a three dimensional picture. The sandtray becomes a stage on which we act out processes of dramatic play.
Worlds of experience are brought to light through the scenes you create. Figures and scenes can express unresolved feelings or internal conflicts, as well as life situations and interpersonal conflicts. Your sandtray world can reveal your own metaphors.
Sandtray is a non-verbal tool that allows a client to use metaphor to articulate subconscious thoughts and beliefs, and internal struggles or desires. Personal experience is transformed into a three dimensional space that therapy uses for discovery.
Sandtray is particularly useful when it is hard to find words to express deep emotional pain, such as grief or trauma. It can be beneficial to any clients feeling "stuck". Sandtray therapy facilitates therapeutic movement, in which clients overcome their own resistance,
let go of defenses, and move forward.
