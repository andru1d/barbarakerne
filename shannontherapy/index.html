therapy
Everyone experiences dissatisfaction, struggle, loss, grief, and crisis at one time or another. You are likely seeking therapy because something is difficult in your life. That "something" can come from inner struggle, a need to be more, a feeling of dissatisfaction, or a life event.
Your children may be acting out. You may be struggling to communicate. You may feel you're losing your self.
I work with people from diverse backgrounds who come to therapy for many reasons.
I strive to create a validating relationship, a foundation of acceptance, so my clients can explore, discover and grow personally. I focus on innate strengths. YOU have all the resources within to find your own best answers. My clinical training, theoretical knowledge, and experience help me guide you on your journey.
My aim is to be strong and present while you reach the center of your issues; to create a safe, therapeutic environment; to foster an easy-going atmosphere in which all can grow, change, and co-create a happier life.
