families [services]
Family Therapy can be helpful when family members aren't getting along, disagree or argue often, or when a child or teen is having behavior problems. My treatment focuses on problem-solving techniques to help each parent re-establish her/his role as an authority figure, while leaving room for consensus and collaboration with your children. Parents and kids both work to examine and change negative behavior patterns.
Family therapy involves counseling sessions with some, or all family members, healing problematic interaction patterns, and helping to improve communication skills among them. Family dynamics and history are explored. Family strengths are built upon.
The results we seek are increased understanding and empathy, more cooperation, and increased satisfaction in family life. I work to assist each family in learning healthy emotional expression and communication styles.
