college students [services]
Going to college is exciting. You experience many new things. This can be challenging!
  • One might find that while high school was relatively easy, and being at the top of the class was not that difficult, it is quite another thing in college.
    Hopes for medical school or another particular career may seem difficult to accomplish.
  • Maybe you have discovered that you are an artist, writer or other career far from what your parents expect of you.
  • You may also be grappling with your sexual orientation.
  • Maybe it isn't school work that is troubling you, but your social life.
    Maybe you are feeling rejected and left out. Finding the right friends isn't always easy.
  • Through changes, maybe friends from freshmen year aren't who you connect with now.
  • Maybe you have been impacted by a life crisis such as your parents' divorce, a death in the family, or other life stressors.
    • If you are suffering from depression or anxiety, my goal will be to help you find your way back to feeling yourself through therapy. Early adulthood is a time when mental illness can take hold, such as bipolar, or schizophrenia. I can help you be properly diagnosed, and collaborate with a physician for your medication treatment. Other school and medical resources can also be accessed with my help.
    I love counseling young adults. It is a time of great discovery. With this can come psychological growing pains unique to you and to your age. I am interested to know who you are, and who you are struggling to become. I relish this time with you. I hope to facilitate your growth.
