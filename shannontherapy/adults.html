adults [services]
People often enter counseling because they are hurting, frustrated, or feeling overwhelmed by a problem. My work with adults ranges from long term psycho-dynamic therapy with in-depth exploration of interpersonal relationships, to short term problem-solving interventions that result in behavioral changes.
Stress, a culprit, takes many forms. People cope with or do not cope with stress in myriad ways. Emotional and spiritual stress can injure us to the core.
As human beings, we try to cope. We mask emotional and psychological suffering through a stew of symptoms, and dysfunctional ways of interacting. Suffering can become too unbearable. Symptoms can debilitate a healthy life style. People seek counseling to rebuild healthy lives.
Problems include: life changes, stress related to work, personal or family life; self or spiritual neglect; anxiety, depression or anger; past trauma or abuse that effects our present state of mind; deficient self-esteem or emotional safety in current relationships; severe mental illness; life threatening illnesses; loss and grief; dealing with anxiety, depression, drug and alcohol addiction; life changes; and relationship problems.
We will work together to figure out what is and isn't working in your life and relationships, and how to create and practice healthy behaviors. My goal is to help you (re)find your strengths and build on them.
